Our Story

A family house, four decades on

Run by the Patterson family, The Pheasant has been built on more than 45 years of genuine hospitality and seasonal cooking.

From relaxed countryside lunches to private celebrations, the house style is warm, attentive and quietly polished — the kind of place where regulars become family and a Sunday table is a tradition worth keeping.
